Spell's

The Harry Potter series features a wide array of spells, each with unique effects and purposes. Notable spells include "Expelliarmus" for disarming opponents, "Lumos" for light, and "Expecto Patronum" for conjuring a protective Patronus. For a comprehensive list, you can explore various resources that detail the spells and their uses.

Book's

The Harry Potter series, written by British author J.K. Rowling, consists of seven fantasy novels that chronicle the life and adventures of a young wizard named Harry Potter. The series is set in a magical world filled with witches, wizards, magical creatures, and various spells.

Character's

Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry has four Houses: Gryffindor, Hufflepuff, Ravenclaw, and Slytherin. Each House has unique traits: Gryffindor values bravery, Hufflepuff values loyalty, Ravenclaw values intelligence, and Slytherin values ambition.

House's

Hogwarts School has four Houses: Gryffindor, Hufflepuff, Ravenclaw, and Slytherin, each with distinct values. Gryffindor champions bravery, Hufflepuff emphasizes loyalty, Ravenclaw prizes intelligence, and Slytherin focuses on ambition.
